Journal subscriptions

Subscriptions are for a calendar year and a complete volume. By subscribing directly with Edinburgh University Press, you or your institution will retain perpetual access to the content published in that subscription year.

Individuals

Individual subscriptions to Edinburgh University Press journals are available as print only, online only or print plus online. An online subscription to the current year's content includes free access to all issues going back to 2005.

Ordering information and prices for individual journals can be found on each journal's subscribe page. To view this information go to the journal homepage you are interested in and click on subscribe/renew.

Institutions

Institutional subscriptions to Edinburgh University Press journals are available as online only or print plus online. An online subscription to the current year's content includes free access to all issues going back to 2005. Edinburgh University Press operates a tiered pricing model for its journal subscriptions.

View our institutional journal subscriptions page.
