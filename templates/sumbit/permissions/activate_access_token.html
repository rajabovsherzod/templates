Activate an access token

Access tokens give you unlimited access to journal content online for a period of time, and will help you determine if a subscription is for you.

How to start your online access:
  1. Create an account or log in to your account
  2. If you are creating a new account, you will receive an automatic email after submitting your details â€“ click on the link in the email to confirm your registration
  3. Once you are logged in, click on the Access Tokens tab, enter your token code and click Submit
  4. Whenever you are logged in, you can read and download articles from the journal for free

Access tokens can only be used once per account. If your access token has expired but you would still like to read the journal, recommend a subscription to your library or take out an individual subscription.
