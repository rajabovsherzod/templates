Cite an article

The bibliographic details for each article can be downloaded to a citation manager, allowing the user to cite the article in their own work. To view or download a citation, please visit any article page and click the ‘View citations’ icon or the Metrics and Citations tab of the article sidebar.

Registered users can opt to track citations of multiple articles by email or RSS feed, so that they receive notification when a selected article is cited. Simply select the relevant article (or articles) on a journal issue Table of Contents page and choose ‘Track Citation’ from the Article Tools drop-down menu.
